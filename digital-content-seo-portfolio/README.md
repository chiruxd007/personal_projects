# Digital Content, SEO & AI Visibility Portfolio

This portfolio is an independent case-study project designed to demonstrate digital content strategy, SEO, generative engine optimisation (GEO), analytics thinking, customer experience and conversion-focused content planning.

It is built for energy-retail style content challenges: helping customers understand energy usage, compare options, reduce confusion, complete self-service actions and discover trusted content through both search engines and AI answer platforms.

## What this portfolio demonstrates

- Content roadmap planning for a consumer energy brand
- Search-intent led blog and landing page strategy
- SEO content briefs with metadata, structure, FAQs and internal linking
- GEO / LLM visibility thinking for AI Overviews, ChatGPT-style tools and answer engines
- GA4 measurement planning, event tracking logic and reporting framework
- CRO and self-service content thinking
- Stakeholder-friendly communication for SMEs, marketing, web, performance and customer teams

## How I would use this in a real role

1. Start with customer problems and search intent, not just keywords.
2. Build topic clusters around recurring customer questions.
3. Structure content so humans, Google and AI systems can understand it clearly.
4. Work with internal subject-matter experts to improve accuracy and authority.
5. Define success metrics before publishing.
6. Use GA4 and search visibility data to improve the roadmap over time.
7. Connect content to business outcomes such as quote starts, self-service completion, reduced support confusion and customer trust.
