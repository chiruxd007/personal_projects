# Sample Social Content Calendar

Independent case-study calendar for Nintendo Australia-style owned digital channels. This is designed to show planning, localisation, scheduling discipline and channel-fit thinking.

## Calendar principles

- Respect global campaign timing and approved messaging.
- Adapt tone and format for Australian audiences without changing the campaign meaning.
- Keep copy accurate, friendly, concise and brand-safe.
- Match the format to the channel: short hooks for social, clearer utility for Switch News, and warmer community language for owned updates.
- Build anticipation without over-promising.
- Monitor fan response and capture useful feedback for weekly reporting.

## Example weekly plan

| Day | Channel | Content type | Audience | Purpose | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Instagram / Facebook | Weekly game spotlight | Families and casual players | Drive awareness and friendly discovery | Use accessible language and clear platform availability |
| Tuesday | X / Facebook | Reminder post | Existing fans | Support campaign timing | Keep copy short, direct and aligned with global wording |
| Wednesday | Switch News | Feature update article | Switch owners | Explain key features and next steps | Include clear headline, image direction and CTA |
| Thursday | Instagram Stories | Poll / interactive sticker | Younger and engaged fans | Encourage interaction | Safe, playful question tied to game world or character |
| Friday | Instagram / TikTok-style short copy | Weekend play moment | Broad audience | Build excitement and shareability | Use fun, visual-first copy |
| Saturday | Community monitoring | Comment review | All followers | Brand safety and fan care | Flag issues, repeated questions and sentiment trends |
| Sunday | Internal report | Weekly performance summary | Marketing stakeholders | Capture learnings | Include activity, engagement, commentary and recommended actions |

## Example campaign week: Adventure game spotlight

### Monday - Announcement reminder

**Channel:** Facebook / Instagram  
**Objective:** Remind local audience about a globally scheduled game update or feature.  
**Copy direction:** Clear, sincere and excitement-led.

> A new adventure awaits on Nintendo Switch. Explore, solve, discover and make the journey your own.

**CTA:** Learn more / Available on Nintendo eShop.

### Wednesday - Switch News update

**Channel:** Switch News  
**Objective:** Give Switch owners a more detailed explanation.  
**Content direction:** Utility-first.

Suggested structure:

1. What is new
2. Why players might enjoy it
3. How to access it
4. Related game or eShop link
5. Family-friendly reminder where needed

### Friday - Weekend play post

**Channel:** Instagram / Facebook  
**Objective:** Create weekend relevance.

> Your weekend quest starts here. Whether you have five minutes or five hours, there is always one more secret to find.

### Sunday - Reporting notes

Track:

- Post engagement rate
- Saves and shares
- Common fan questions
- Sentiment patterns
- Link clicks where available
- Content that may need clarification
- Potential follow-up post ideas

## Localisation checklist

Before scheduling:

- Is the date/time correct for Australia?
- Does the copy follow approved global campaign direction?
- Is spelling aligned to Australian English where appropriate?
- Are platform names, game titles and trademarks correct?
- Is the CTA accurate?
- Are assets matched to the correct channel format?
- Has copy been proofread for tone, clarity and consistency?
- Are moderation notes ready for expected fan questions?
