# Nintendo Australia Content Marketing Portfolio

Independent application portfolio for a Content Marketing Coordinator role. This is a fan-informed case-study project and is not affiliated with Nintendo.

The portfolio demonstrates content calendar coordination, localisation thinking, channel-fit copywriting, proofreading, community moderation, weekly reporting and campaign planning for Nintendo Australia-style digital channels.

## Why this portfolio exists

Nintendo content is different from ordinary marketing content. The tone needs to feel playful, precise, safe, family-friendly, brand-consistent and respectful of a global campaign calendar while still feeling locally relevant for Australian audiences.

This portfolio shows how I would approach that work as someone who loves Nintendo worlds, understands digital channels, and can bring technical organisation, reporting and content discipline to a marketing team.

## My Nintendo content philosophy

Nintendo's strength is not just hardware. It is originality, emotional memory, family connection and worlds that people return to across generations. Great content should protect that feeling while making each post, update and announcement clear, timely and useful.
